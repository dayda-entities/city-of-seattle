This dataset lists wheelchair accessible taxicab (WAT) owners and drivers who received reimbursements from the Wheelchair Accessible Services Fund for wheelchair passenger trips made between January 1 and December 30, 2018. The reimbursement amount is $20 per wheelchair passenger trip.
